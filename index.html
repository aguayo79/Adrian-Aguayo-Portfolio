About Me

My name is Adrian Aguayo, and I am a game designer with a passion for creating immersive and engaging gaming experiences. I have a background in computer science and a strong interest in storytelling, which I believe are essential elements in game design. I enjoy collaborating with artists, programmers, and other designers to bring my ideas to life. In my free time, I like to play video games, read about the latest trends in the industry, and experiment with new game mechanics.
